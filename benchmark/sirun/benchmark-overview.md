# sirun benchmark overview

Local macOS, 6 samples/variant (overview-grade, not the CI gate). wall.time is microseconds in sirun; reported per-iteration in ms. "total" = mean x configured iterations. "startup%" = load+setup share from the guard.

Live client/server benches (appsec, appsec-iast, http, net, debugger) are network/scheduler noisy locally, so their stddev here is not meaningful -- CI with core pinning is authoritative. startup-time variants need /opt/insecure-bank-js (a CI-only clone) and show "error" locally.
